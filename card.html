Monarch Butterfly

Every year the Monarch Butterfly migrates an estimated of 3,000 miles towards the same destination as its ancestors.

  • Scientific Name: Danaus Plexippus
  • Average Length: 3-5 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 2-6 weeks, with the excepeiton of the last generation of the year, some living up to 9 months.
  • Habitat: Prairies/Meadows

The Monarch Butterfly has 2 beautiful bright orange wings, with a wingspan of 7 - 13cm, with white spots that warn predators that it is poisonous. Its poison derives from the milkweeds they feed on. Upon migrating, some Monarchs visit an old tree or location as its previous ancestors.
